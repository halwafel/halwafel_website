The Frame Workshop, the place to be

 

This website gathers every piece of information about The Frame Workshop by Halwafel. For those who wonder, The Frame Workshop is a 3D Animation software that offers different tools to make animations with preset or self-made meshes. It tends to be more user-friendly than other softwares of this kind such as MikuMikuDance that everyone surely knows.

Why a workshop ?

Not everyone gets the chance to own a workshop, so here it is. Here you can organize your own workshop's space, put your tools wherever you want and do whatever you want with them. Also, it is a place where you can find peace and take time to think, nothing is better than that to let creativity be !
